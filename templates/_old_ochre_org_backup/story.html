Our Story

We're not riding the wave. We're the tide that moves it.

Crafting Excellence

From our humble beginnings to becoming a recognized name in craft spirits, our journey has been driven by passion and innovation. We believe in pushing boundaries while respecting the art of distillation.

Every bottle tells a story of dedication, precision, and creativity. Our master distillers combine traditional techniques with modern innovation to create spirits that stand out in both flavor and character.

We source the finest ingredients and employ sustainable practices throughout our production process. Quality isn't just a goal—it's our foundation, our promise, and our legacy.

—OUR PHILOSOPHY—

Inclusivity Over Exclusivity

1

Accessible Excellence

We believe exceptional spirits should be enjoyed by everyone, not reserved for the elite few. Quality without pretension is our promise.

Our Journey

2023

The Beginning

Ochre Spirits launches in Goa with our first collection of craft spirits, introducing Saffron Gin, Peach & Cherry Vodka, and our signature flavored rums.

2024

Expansion

Introduction of new flavors including Rose & Raspberry Gin, Kiwi & Pear Gin, and fruit-forward vodkas. Opening of India's first-ever brand tasting room.

2025

Innovation Continues

Launch of premium Saffron Vodka and continued commitment to creating spirits that transcend boundaries and celebrate tradition with a modern twist.
